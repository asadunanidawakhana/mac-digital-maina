# 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

## Project Name

**Mac Digital Agency**

## Product Vision

Mac Digital Agency is a premium single-page digital agency portfolio website designed to establish credibility, showcase creative capabilities, and convert visitors into qualified business leads. The website represents the agency's identity through modern visual storytelling, elegant layouts, polished animations, and a carefully crafted user experience.

Unlike generic portfolio templates, this website should feel handcrafted, highly refined, and visually memorable while remaining lightweight, fast, and easy to maintain.

---

## Project Purpose

The website exists to:

* Present the agency as a premium service provider.
* Build trust through professional branding.
* Showcase completed work.
* Explain services clearly.
* Demonstrate technical and creative expertise.
* Encourage visitors to request quotations.
* Generate consultation inquiries.
* Serve as the agency's official online identity.

---

## Primary Goals

* Increase lead generation.
